What is WebMiggle?

WebMiggle is your digital companion for the Miggle Light smart notebook. Miggle Light is a purely analog device—no screens, no batteries, no cloud, and no distractions. Enjoy the simplicity of pen and paper, enhanced with thoughtful organization on the web.

Stay Focused

No screens, no notifications, just pure writing. Miggle Light keeps you in the moment.

Organize

Use WebMiggle to tag, search, and organize your handwritten notes after you digitize them.

Analog Simplicity

No batteries, no charging, no cloud—just your ideas, on paper, at your pace.

Get Started

  1. Write your notes in Miggle Light.
  2. Digitize your notes using your preferred method (scan or photo).
  3. Upload and organize them with WebMiggle.
